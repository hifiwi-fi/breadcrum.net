Breadcrum offers a free tier and a paid subscription.

## Free tier

- 10 new bookmarks per month.
- Quota resets on the 1st of each month (UTC).
- Access to all existing bookmarks and core features.

## Paid subscription

- Unlimited bookmarks.
- Billed yearly.
- Bookmark import (when available).

## Billing

- Payments are handled by [Stripe](https://stripe.com).
- You can subscribe, cancel, and manage your billing from your [account settings](/account/).
- Canceling your subscription prevents future charges. You keep access to paid features until the end of your current billing period.
- See our [billing and refund policy](/legal/billing/) for full details.
